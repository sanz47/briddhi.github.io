Different Business Units

Commodity

Commodity is a B2B business where ShopUp connects large suppliers, mills, and factories with retailers, streamlining the supply chain to meet a significant portion of essential commodity needs. ShopUp acts as a crucial link, ensuring smooth and efficient distribution.

CPG

CPG is a distributorship business where ShopUp distributes fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG) to retailers, with suppliers referred to as anchors. It ensures efficient delivery of goods from these anchors to retail outlets ensuring smooth and efficient distribution.

Telco

Telco is a SIM company distributorship where ShopUp manages balance loading and SIM sales, taking responsibility for efficiently distributing telecom products to retailers.

Remedist

Remedist is the national distributor for MamaEarth and Emami, responsible for distributing these skincare products through ShopUp's network to the retailers.

Syngenta

Syngenta is a seller of pesticides and fertilizers, providing retailers with agricultural products. It ensures timely delivery to support efficient farming and successful harvesting.

RedX 3PL

RedX 3PL(third-party logistics) is a service that delivers goods for businesses that lack their own delivery systems. It provides end-to-end logistics solutions, ensuring reliable delivery for third-party clients.

RedX FTL

RedX FTL (Full Truck Load) provides logistics support to suppliers requiring the transport of a full truckload of products, ensuring efficient and reliable large-scale deliveries.

RedX RAP

RedX RAP provides loans to 3PL merchants, offering financial support to enhance their logistics capabilities. This helps merchants manage operations efficiently.

Loan

Loans are provided based on tenure, such as 120 days, with PAR 30 indicating that 30 days have passed since the loan's tenure completion. This helps assess the loan's performance and repayment status.

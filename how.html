Working Procedure of 5 Types of Flowcharts

Commodity

This flowchart outlines a decision-making process starting with a question and leading to conditional branches with an action based on the decision.

CPG

This flowchart shows the cycle of product development, beginning from idea generation and leading to prototyping, testing, and launching.

Telco

This flowchart represents the customer support workflow starting from inquiries, troubleshooting, and ending with resolution or escalation.

Remedist

The software development lifecycle flowchart covers phases like planning, designing, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance.

Syngenta

This flowchart explains the data analysis process from collection, cleaning, analysis, visualization, and insights to decision-making.
